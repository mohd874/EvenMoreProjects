Scope:

- the system will help the faculty to select the unused hours for a classrooms and reserve it

- the reservation officer will accept/reject the reservation request based on the business logic

- the system will generate reports for the classrooms. e.g. room schedule

- the system will check both banner and local databases for validation to avoid the overlapping

- the security will be role based

- the system will send a notifcation to the faculty(accept/decline)

- the system will allow the officer to delete a reservation after accepting it.
